Government College of Technology, Coimbatore, popularly known as GCT Coimbatore, is a state-funded Engineering college that was established in 1945. Formerly known as Arthur Hope College of Technology, the college is affiliated to Anna University and accredited by the NBA and the NAAC with a Grade “A”

  1. Government College of Technology, Coimbatore was ranked 90 among engineering colleges by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) in 2019
  2. In 2020, it has been ranked 104 among engineering institutes in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework
  3. Government College of Technology, Coimbatore categorized as 'Band A' Institution (rank between 06-25) in category of institutes and colleges (Govt. and Govt. aided) by Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovative Achievements in 2020
