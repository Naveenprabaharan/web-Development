Programmes:

To promote innovation, inspire research, educate and support new innovations and create environments where students can succeed.

Under Graduate Programmes:


GCT, Coimbatore offers 9 Under Graduate ( UG) Courses 4 of which are also available as part-time courses.[1] Candidates are selected in the undergraduate programme on the basis of the TNEA score. Admission to all the undergraduate programme is done through the single window system of Anna University.

Post Graduate Programmes:


The Institute provides Full-time Post Graduate (PG) Courses under various specializations. Candidates are selected in the ME/ MTech programme on the basis of TANCET/GATE score. Candidates are selected in the postgraduate programme through Anna University Single Window System. Candidate must have a valid TANCET/GATE score to get eligible for the programme.
